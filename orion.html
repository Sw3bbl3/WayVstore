New Product

Orion

Orion is a modern, open-source digital audio workstation built for performance, simplicity, and creators.

Orion is in active development and evolving quickly. We are focusing on the core audio experience first, while building the architecture needed for long-term extensibility.

Follow Development

Current Focus

  • • Real-time audio processing and low-latency playback
  • • Multi-track timeline for MIDI and audio workflows
  • • Mixer and routing foundations
  • • Fast, minimal user interface with real-time feedback

Planned / In Progress

  • • Plugin ecosystem support (including external plugin integration)
  • • Internal plugin architecture for community extensions
  • • Deeper optimization for larger and more complex projects
  • • Broader cross-platform support over time
