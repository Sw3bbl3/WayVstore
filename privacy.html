Privacy Policy

At WayV Software Inc., we build products that respect your privacy by design. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can stay in control.

Last updated: January 2025

Information we collect

  • Account details such as your email when you sign up for betas or newsletters.
  • Device and diagnostic data that helps us improve performance and stability.
  • Support correspondence that you choose to share when contacting our team.

How we use information

  • To provide and improve our apps, services, and platform experiences.
  • To communicate updates such as beta releases, feature announcements, or policy changes.
  • To protect users, prevent abuse, and maintain the security of WayV systems.

Data control and choices

  • You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link.
  • You may request access, updates, or deletion of your personal data by contacting privacy@wayvsoftware.com.
  • We do not sell personal information. Data is only shared with service providers that support our operations.

Data retention and security

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. We use encryption, strict access controls, and regular audits to safeguard the information entrusted to us.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle data, please reach out to privacy@wayvsoftware.com.
