April 3, 2026 · Product Announcement

Orion joins the WayV product lineup

We are introducing Orion, a modern open-source DAW focused on low-latency audio, a clean interface, and a modular architecture. Orion is currently in active development, and we are clearly separating what is available now from what is planned next.

  • Current focus: real-time audio engine, multi-track timeline, MIDI/audio workflows, and mixer foundations.
  • Planned / in progress: plugin ecosystem support, expanded internal extensions, and broader platform coverage.
